How is PlastikoPatch made?

Various items that can be used: Plastic bags, plastic cups, plastic packaging used for potato chips, biscuits, chocolates etc.

Plastic waste is shredded to a particular size using a shredding machine.
The shredded plastic waste is then added to aggregate
The plastic waste-coated aggregate is mixed with hot bitumen and the resulting mix is used for road construction.
