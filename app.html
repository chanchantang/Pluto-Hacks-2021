What is Shravan?

Shravan is an elegantly thought out and well-planned user interface that does not require the sense of sight–making it perfect for those with impaired or reduced vision.

Easily visible, high contrast buttons that can be discerned with ease.
Auditory and haptic feedback on all boxes lets you know where you are all the time.
The interface can be customized by the mentor, providing you the experience you want.
