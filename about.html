Why PlastikoPatch?

PlastikoPatch is an elegantly thought out solution to recycle locally generated plastic waste, using it to fill the roads and not precious land.

Increased integrity of road. No stripping or potholes
Better resistance to water and water stagnation; more load withstanding capacity
Less carbon emissions and less UV radiation compared to asphalt
